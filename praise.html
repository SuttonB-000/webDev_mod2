The Prayer of Chuck Norris

O mighty Chuck, guardian of the fearless, Whose gaze alone bends the laws of man and nature, Grant us the strength of your fists and the swiftness of your roundhouse,

That we may face life’s foes with courage unshaken.

Blessed are those who follow your path of justice, For they shall never stub a toe nor miss a high-five. Deliver us from cowardice, from mediocrity, and from poorly timed ninja attacks,

And lead us, by the way of your bearded wisdom, to triumph in all endeavors.

In the name of the Kick, the Beard, and the Eternal Power of Awesome,

We pray.

Amen.
